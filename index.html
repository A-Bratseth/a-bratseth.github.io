About Me

Originally from Trondheim, Norway, Adrian began his professional journey in creative storytelling—producing advertising films for local businesses. This early passion for visual communication culminated in a documentary film, that was featured at the kosmorama Film Festival in 2018, marking a proud milestone in his creative pursuits.

Eager to broaden his horizons, Adrian moved to Singapore in 2019 to pursue a Bachelor's degree in International Business. It didn’t take long for him to gravitate toward a specialization in banking and finance, where he discovered a deep interest in the mechanics of global markets.

This growing fascination led him to Ireland in 2022, where he joined Bloomberg LP as a quantitative data processor. There, he contributed to the delivery of accurate and timely corporate financial information to investors worldwide via the Bloomberg Terminal—one of the most influential tools in modern finance.

In 2024, driven by a desire to deepen his understanding of the international business landscape, Adrian founded a series of 2 companies in Germany. In these ventures, he has worn multiple hats, working as a financial controller, FP&A analyst, and marketing and sales coordinator. These diverse roles have given him hands-on insight into both the strategic and operational sides of business, further fueling his ambition to play a meaningful role in shaping economic decision-making.
